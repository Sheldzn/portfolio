About Me

I am a South African lad, born and bred, from the small coastal city of Durban...which many consider paradise. As a young boy and teenager I was sport mad and did every sport that school and clubs allowed from soccer and cricket to fishing and golfing. I was also crazy about computers and more specifically, gaming. Not sure what I wanted to do I started at University doing a BA and came across a couple Computer Science modules which I loved. I went on to finish my BA degree and Honours before interning at an agency where I started teaching myself programming. A year or 2 later I went on to get a diploma in computer science, more as a formality. Since then I have been at 1 other agency and have consistently been upskilling and mastering new skills.
