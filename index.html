About Me

My name is Sheldon and I am Professional Developer with over 7+ years of experience working with Shopify, Webflow and Wordpress. Having years of experience in developing, customizing and modifying mainly ecommerce websites, I exist to help develop and optimise Shopify and Shopify Plus, Webflow and Wordpress websites for both local and international.

I have had the privilege of building and working on brands such as Salomon SA, Jockey SA, Boardriders (Quiksilver, Roxy, DC Shoes, RVCA), Le Coq Sportif, CUM Books and many many more. I have built some backend apps that make my life easier when maintaining Ecommerce sites, such as bulk price changers, auto order and customer tagger. They are not for commercial use but to help in the day to day running of Shopify sites. I love to code and build websites and spend as much time possible learning the latest trends and fine tuning my techniques until I am the best!
